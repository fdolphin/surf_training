Key principles
Lower back
No heavy spinal loading in phase 1. Favour hip hinge, anti-rotation and anti-extension core work. If pain spikes, drop to bodyweight only.
Hamstring history
All hinge movements: 3-sec eccentric. No ballistic hamstring loading before week 5. Nordic curls introduced conservatively in phase 3.
Fasting days
No gym on 36hr fast days. Don't schedule a session within 12hrs of ending a fast.
Surf & gym rhythm
Don't lift heavy the day before a big surf. Day C follows a surf day. At least one full rest day per week.
